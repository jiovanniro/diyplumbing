About the Course

This comprehensive hands-on training course is designed specifically for homeowners who want to learn basic plumbing skills. Taught by a licensed journeyman plumber with professional experience, you'll gain practical knowledge to handle common household plumbing issues on your own property.

Training takes place in a customized garage workshop, professionally designed for learning with real plumbing fixtures and materials. You'll get actual hands-on experience with the tools and techniques you'll use at home.

This course is designed for homeowner education and personal residence use only. It does not provide professional licensing or certification.
